# Paper Evaluation Assignment (IK2221)

**Format requirements**
- Max 1 page.
- Minimum 12pt font.
- Margins: your choice.
- Submit as PDF.
- No references required.

**Header (top of page)**
- Course: IK2221 + term
- Paper title
- Your name (no personal number) and email

---

## Contents

Use **only these numbered sections**.  
Each section must be **one paragraph** (no bullet points).  
Aim for **~1/3 page per section**.  
Write complete, thoughtful comments (assume authors will read them).  
Complaints about readability do **not** count as issues.

### 1. Paper Summary (E level)
Include:
- Problem being tackled and why it is difficult  
- Key idea behind the approach  
- Some additional detail on how the approach works  
- How the idea is evaluated and key findings  

### 2. Top 3 Contributions (C level)
- Tie each contribution to its impact  
- Examples of phrasing:
  - The first paper to propose…  
  - Makes it possible to…  
  - Presents a new technique for…  
  - Dramatically improves performance of…  
  - Offers largely improved reliability…  
  - Saves a large fraction of power for…  
- Note: Some papers explicitly list contributions  

### 3. Most Glaring Problems (A level)
Up to 3 issues. Do **not** consult external sources.

Consider:
- Scalability (e.g., non-linear resource growth with users)  
- Behavior under failures  
- Missing important cases  
- Overly strong assumptions  
- Unrealistic deployment requirements  

---

## Code of Honor

It is essential to follow the KTH EECS Code of Honor in all assignments.

Plagiarism includes:
- Using others’ ideas or material without acknowledgment  
- Presenting others’ work as your own  
- Copying text (even with small modifications) without disclosure  

Guidelines:
- Write in your own words  
- Use quotation marks when directly quoting any source  
- Clearly indicate any borrowed material  

**Expectation:** Your submission should reflect your personal understanding and evaluation of the paper.
